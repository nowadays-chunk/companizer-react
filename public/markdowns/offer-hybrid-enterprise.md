# Hybrid / Enterprise Offer
## The Best of Both Worlds

The **Hybrid / Enterprise** plan is our bespoke solution for large-scale operations that require the flexibility of the cloud combined with the security of on-premise systems. It allows for a federated architecture where sensitive financial data can remain on-premise while operational data lives in the cloud.

### Commercial Overview
*   **Pricing Model**: Custom Quote (Volume Licensing).
*   **Ideal For**: Multinational Corporations, Agencies, and Conglomerates.
*   **Account Management**: Dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM).

### Technical Specifications
*   **Architecture**: Federated Grid. connect multiple Self-Hosted nodes with Cloud nodes into a single unified dashboard.
*   **Identity Management**: Single Sign-On (SSO) with Okta, Azure AD, or Custom SAML.
*   **Audit**: Advanced Audit Logs with forensic capabilities and 7-year retention.
*   **White Labeling**: Full UI customization to match your corporate brand identity.

### Included Features
*   **Unlimited Scale**: No hard limits on Organizations or Entities.
*   **Development**: Access to Beta features and a dedicated sandbox environment.
*   **Support**: 24/7 Dedicated Phone & Slack Support (1h SLA).
*   **Onboarding**: Guided implementation and team training sessions.

### Why Choose Hybrid?
*   **Complex Workflows**: Map your complex organizational structure (Holding companies, subsidiaries) exactly as it is.
*   **Risk Mitigation**: Diversify deployment targets to minimize rigid dependency on a single cloud provider.
*   **Brand Unity**: Make the ERP feel like your own proprietary internal tool.
