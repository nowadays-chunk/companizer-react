# StartUp Cloud Offer
## The Agile Choice for Growing Teams

The **StartUp Cloud** plan is designed for modern businesses that prioritize speed, ease of use, and minimal maintenance overhead. Hosted on our secure, high-performance global infrastructure, this plan allows you to focus on your business while we handle the technical complexity.

### Commercial Overview
*   **Pricing Model**: Per User / Month.
*   **Ideal For**: Startups, Small to Medium Businesses (SMBs), and agile teams.
*   **Commitment**: Monthly or Annual (15% discount on annual).

### Technical Specifications
*   **Infrastructure**: Multi-tenant architecture on AWS/Azure (Region selectable).
*   **Uptime SLA**: 99.9% guaranteed uptime with automated failover.
*   **Updates**: Automatic, zero-downtime rolling updates. You are always on the latest version.
*   **Security**:
    *   AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit.
    *   Automated daily backups with 30-day retention.
    *   SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure.

### Included Features
*   **Users**: Scalable from 1 to 200 users.
*   **Organizations**: Limit of 1 Organization entity per subscription.
*   **Support**: Standard Email Support (24h response time).
*   **Modules**: Access to all Core modules (Finance, HR, Operations). Premium modules available as add-ons.

### Why Choose Cloud?
*   **Zero operational overhead**: No servers to manage.
*   **Rapid Deployment**: Go live in minutes, not days.
*   **Cost Efficiency**: Pay only for what you use with our elastic billing model.
